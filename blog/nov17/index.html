Next Hacky Hour at UQ

SH

2015-11-06 00:00

Source

The next Hacky Hour at St Lucia (The University of Queensland) will be on Tuesday, 17 November, from 4-5pm.

Come down to St Lucy's cafe and say hi.

You don't need to have a problem that needs solving - you might just want to touch base for later.

Or you might want to learn how to do something like ... start up a virtual machine on the Australian research cloud, install some software on your laptop, or find out about the next Software Carpentry training event or about local meetups, like COMBINE for bioinformaticians.

We look forward to meeting you.

-- Belinda and Sam
